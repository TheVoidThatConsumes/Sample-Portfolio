ABOUT ME.

I am Obi David, a creative writer and budding fullstack developer in my early twenties.
I have been writing longer than I have been coding, but both paths share the same common drive within me: Creativity.
Facing code poses more of a challenge to me than facing a story beginning in the middle of the plot, but does it matter?
In the end, "we must embrace infinity."
Just as it is.
